Who is Sabrina Carpenter?

Sabrina Carpenter is an American singer, songwriter, and actress born in 1999 in Lehigh Valley, Pennsylvania. Known for her versatile pop sound, powerful vocals, her ability to blend storytelling with catchy melodies in her music, and playful lyrics.

Visit Sabrina Carpenter's website

Sabrina Carpenter’s beginnings

Sabrina Carpenter began acting at a young age, gaining the most recognition for her role as Maya on Disney Channel’s Girl Meets World in 2014. While working as an actress, she also pursued music, signing with Hollywood Records. She released her debut album, Eyes Wide Open, in 2015, which showcased her pop style and marked the beginning of her music career.

From actress to singer

Since beginning her music career, Sabrina Carpenter has become a rising figure in the pop industry. She has embarked on several headlining tours, performing across North America, Europe, and Asia, and has also appeared at major music festivals. Her songs have gained viral popularity on streaming platforms and social media, especially with younger audiences who connect with her honest and playful lyricism. In addition to her music, Sabrina has starred in films and television projects and partnered with global brands like Prada, establishing herself as both a successful performer and a versatile entertainer.
